### Objective
To analyze survey responses from data professionals and identify trends
in job roles, salaries, programming languages, and job satisfaction.

### Dataset
Survey data collected from 630 data professionals across different countries,
roles, and experience levels.

### Tools & Skills
- Power BI
- Power Query (data cleaning & transformation)
- DAX (calculated measures)
- Data Visualization & Storytelling

### Key Insights
- Python is the most preferred programming language across all roles
- Data Scientists and Data Engineers report the highest average salaries
- Higher education levels generally correlate with higher salary ranges
- Overall satisfaction with work-life balance is higher than salary satisfaction
- Majority of respondents are Data Analysts

### Dashboard Features
- Interactive visuals with role and country based filtering
- KPI cards for survey count and average age
- Salary analysis by job title and education level
- Satisfaction metrics for work-life balance and salary
